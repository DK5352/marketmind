WHAT IS MARKETMIND?
--------------------
A personal AI wealth manager that runs on your computer. It monitors the stock
market daily, analyses data from 13 different sources, and gives you specific
investment recommendations — all explained in plain English for a beginner.


================================================================================
THE 4 MODES (MAIN MENU)
================================================================================

1. DAILY WEALTH REPORT
-----------------------
Your day-to-day portfolio manager. Every time you run it:
  - Reads your current portfolio (cash + holdings)
  - Screens all stocks in your investment universe
  - Analyses each stock you currently hold
  - Gives specific BUY / SELL / HOLD recommendations with exact share counts
    and prices
  - Tracks whether you are on pace for your weekly return target
  - Flags risks, insider selling, and unusual social activity
  - Saves a dated report to reports/report_YYYY-MM-DD.md


2. STOCK FORECAST
------------------
For when you hear news about a stock and want to know if you should invest.
You type any ticker (e.g. TSLA) and it:
  - Reads all recent news and rates each story:
      Bullish / Bearish / Neutral + LOW / MEDIUM / HIGH / MAJOR impact
  - Checks if the market has already priced in the news
  - Combines news impact with technical signals
  - Gives a verdict: STRONG BUY / BUY / WAIT / AVOID / STRONG AVOID
  - With: confidence score (X/10), entry price, stop-loss, target price,
    and time horizon
  - Ends with a plain-English beginner's summary
  - Saves forecast to reports/forecast_TICKER_YYYY-MM-DD.md


3. TOP 10 US STOCKS SNAPSHOT
------------------------------
A live comparison table of the 10 biggest US companies:
Apple, Microsoft, NVIDIA, Amazon, Alphabet, Meta,
Berkshire Hathaway, Eli Lilly, Broadcom, Tesla

  Column       What it shows
  ----------   -------------------------------------------------------
  Today $      Live market price right now
  Target $     Where Wall Street analysts think it will be in 12 months
  Upside       Gap between today's price and analyst target
  7-Day        How much it moved this week
  AI (date)    MarketMind's predicted price in 7 days
  Conf.        How many technical indicators agree (High/Medium/Low)
  Predict?     How trustworthy the prediction is + beta coefficient
  Rating       Wall Street consensus (Strong Buy to Sell)

Saves to reports/top10_snapshot_YYYY-MM-DD.txt


4. BACKTEST MARKETMIND PREDICTIONS
------------------------------------
Tests how accurate MarketMind would have been last week:
  - Goes back 7 trading days and pretends it is running on that date
  - Uses only data that was available on that date (no cheating)
  - Generates the same predictions it would have made then
  - Compares predicted price vs what actually happened
  - Shows: error %, direction accuracy (did it call UP/DOWN correctly?),
    and an overall accuracy summary
  - Saves to reports/backtest_DATE_to_DATE.txt

================================================================================
PREDICTABILITY SCORING
================================================================================

Before making any prediction, MarketMind scores how reliable that prediction
is based on 30-day historical volatility and beta coefficient.

  Score    Criteria                          What it means
  ------   --------------------------------  -----------------------------------
  High     Volatility < 20% AND beta < 1.2  Stable stock. Technicals are
                                            reliable. Trust the prediction more.
  Medium   Volatility 20-35% or beta 1.2-   Some noise. Prediction is a good
           1.8                              guide but watch for surprises.
  Low      Volatility > 35% or beta > 1.8   High-volatility stock (e.g. Tesla).
                                            News and sentiment can override
                                            technicals at any time.

Why Tesla predictions are less reliable than Apple or Microsoft:
  - Tesla beta is around 2.3 (moves 2.3x the market)
  - 30-day volatility is very high
  - A single tweet or news story can move it more than a full week of
    technical signals would predict


================================================================================
SETUP AND CONFIGURATION
================================================================================

At first run you are asked two questions:

  1. How much to invest — your starting capital (e.g. $10,000)

  2. Risk appetite:
       Conservative  — 1.0% weekly target, stable dividend-paying stocks,
                       capital preservation is the priority
       Moderate      — 2.0% weekly target, mix of stable and growth stocks
       Aggressive    — 3.5% weekly target, momentum and growth stocks,
                       higher risk for higher potential reward

On every return visit it asks if you want to update your risk level.

Investment rules built in:
  - No new money added for 6 months
  - Profits are reinvested (compounding)
  - No single stock can exceed 20% of total portfolio value
  - Stop-loss logic: flag stocks down more than 8% from buy price

HOW IT ALL CONNECTS
================================================================================

  You run stock_friend.py
          |
          v
  Ask risk appetite (first run) or confirm (returning user)
          |
          v
  Choose mode:
    1. Daily Report
    2. Stock Forecast
    3. Top 10 Snapshot
    4. Backtest
          |
          v
  MarketMind fetches data from 13 sources using its built-in tools
          |
          v
  All data is sent to Claude (claude-opus-4-6)
          |
          v
  Claude analyses everything and writes the report
          |
          v
  Report printed to screen + saved to reports/ folder
